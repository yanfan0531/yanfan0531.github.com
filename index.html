I was in the corpus group during the internship in Alibaba, working on bilingual in-domain term induction. The model utilizes training dictionaries to learn a transformation matrix, in order to map the source word embeddings to the target space. We further upgrade the model from single matrix to multiple matrices according to different weights for each training sample. The research will be published on the conference SDM 2019.
